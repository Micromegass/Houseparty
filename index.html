ABOUT HOUSEPARTY

HOUSEPARTY (GER, Raptor Records) is a four-piece partymachine hailing from Nürnberg (GER) fueled by pizza and weißbier. It's stripped down indie-surf-punk, topped with catchy melodies. After their debut-EP “keept it simple” (Raptor Records) from Spring 2018, they will release their debut-album in June 2019. The Band started in summer of 2018 and came out of some other successful projects like garage-pop-punx “No Fun” or indie-rockers “The Fountains”. Being well known for fun life-performances and likable characters, Houseparty played more than 30 shows in only a couple of months after their release “keep it simple” (Raptor Records) in April 2018.

In Spring 2019 they plan to release their first full-length album, head over for a tour in the UK and play loads of festivals in summer. They also supported a good handful of influencial bands like Muncie Girls, Walter etc. and Wonk Unit. The band is available for festival slots in 2019 all around Europe as well as for headline and support shows/tours. Houseparty comes from the DIY-scene and also loves to do house-shows, rehearsal-room-shows or soli-shows for the politically-oppressed. If you can help out with a show, please feel free to hit us up.
