Houseparty (GER, Raptor Records) HOUSEPARTY is a four-piece partymachine hailing from Nürnberg (GER) fueled by pizza and weißbier. It´s stripped down indie-surf-punk, topped with catchy melodies. After their debut-EP “keept it simple” (Raptor Records) from spring 2018, they will release their debut-album in june 2019. The Band started in the summer of 2018 and came out of some other succesfull projects like garage-pop-punx “No Fun” or indie-rockers “The Fountains”. Being well known for fun life-performances and likable characters Houseparty played more than 30 shows in only a couple of months after their release “keep it simple” (Raptor Records) in april 2018. In spring 2019 they plan to release their first full-length album, head over for a tour in the UK and play loads of festivals in the summer. They also supporteåd a good handful of influancal bands like Muncie, Girls, Walter etc. and Wonk Unit. New Single: https://housepartypunk.bandcamp.com/track/arco-di-lido Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8afyD-zrLJY FB: https://www.facebook.com/housepartypunk/ Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/housepartypunk/ The band is available for festival slots 2019 Europe-wide as well as for headline and support shows/tours. Houseparty comes out of the DIY-scene and also loves to do house-shows, practice-room-shows or soli-shows for the political-oppressed. If you can help with a show, please feel free to hit us up.
